Technical Event Rules

Quiz

Team:

maximum 3 members
First round:20 Questions
Second round:10 Questions
Third round: 10 Questions

Questions Based:

Technical:25 questions
General:15 questions

Rules:

1.Don't use mobile phones.
2.Don't discuss with others teams.

Time limits:

one question for one minutes.

UI/UX Design

Duration:

1 hour 30 minutes

Team Size:

Maximum 2 participants

Competition Rules:


1.Mobile phones are Not Allowed
2.All designs must be original, creative, and unique.
3.No external resources or references are permitted during the competition.
4.Participants are free to use any design tool, including Figma, Sketch, and others.
5.Participants may choose to bring their own laptop or utilize the system provided by management.

Rules:

Paper Presentation

7 minutes for the presentation. Q&A Session: 2-3 minutes for questions from judges or the audience.
